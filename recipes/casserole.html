Squash Casserole

Description

Creamy squash casserole with crunchy cracke and cheddar topping

Ingredients

  • 4 cups sliced yellow squash
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 35 round crackers
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and Preheat ovent to 400 degrees
  2. Place squash and onion in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Mix cracker crumbs and cheddar cheese together in separate bowl and place half mixture into cooked squash and onions
  4. Mix eggs and milk together in a small bowl. Add squash mixture and stir 1/4 cup melted butter and season with salt and pepper
  5. Sprinkle with remaining cracker mixture
  6. Bake in the oven until lightly browned (~25 minutes)
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
